Stretch Break

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

The short version: Stretch Break is built to be private. We do not collect, sell, or have access to your personal data. Everything the app records — your settings, breaks, streaks and any Health data — stays on your device and in your own private iCloud. There are no ads and no third-party trackers.

Who we are

Stretch Break (“the app”) is developed by hts (“we”, “us”). This policy explains how the app handles information. If you have any questions, contact us at htariksavas@gmail.com.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. We have no servers and no analytics. The app works entirely on your device. We never see your data.

Information stored on your device

To make the app work, the following is stored locally on your device (and, if you enable iCloud, synced through your own private Apple iCloud account, which we cannot access):

  • Your settings — break interval, chosen exercises, theme, language and reminder preferences.
  • Your activity — completed breaks, daily streak and break history used to show your statistics.

Health data with your permission

If you grant access, the app uses Apple HealthKit to:

  • Read Mindful Minutes, heart rate and heart-rate variability to show your wellness trends.
  • Save your completed breaks as Mindful Minutes.

This Health data is processed on your device and stored in Apple’s Health app. It is never transmitted to us or any third party, and is never used for advertising. You can review or revoke access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Stretch Break.

Calendar, Camera, and Motion optional features

  • Calendar — if enabled, the app reads your calendar only to detect meetings so it can postpone a break. This happens on your device; calendar contents are never stored or sent anywhere.
  • Camera — used only while the optional posture check is open, to detect slouching on your device. No photo or video is ever recorded, saved, or transmitted.
  • Motion — AirPods and device motion are used on your device to give live form feedback during a stretch. Motion data is not stored or transmitted.

Subscriptions and purchases

Stretch Break Pro is an optional subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your name, payment card, or billing details. Apple manages all transactions; please see Apple’s Privacy Policy for how Apple handles purchase information.

No tracking, no advertising

The app contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs. We do not build a profile of you and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

Stretch Break is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email htariksavas@gmail.com.
