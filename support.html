How do break reminders work?

Choose your interval in Settings (for example, every 30 minutes). When a break is due, Stretch Break reminds you and you can follow a quick guided stretch — most take about 60 seconds.

I’m not getting notifications.

Make sure notifications are allowed in iOS Settings → Notifications → Stretch Break. Also check that you’re not inside your Quiet Hours window, and that a Focus isn’t silencing reminders (the app can respect Focus if you turn that on in Settings).

What’s included in Stretch Break Pro?

Pro adds detailed statistics, Calendar integration, Health data insights, the AirPods Form Coach, and the Mac menu-bar app. The timer, all 15 exercises, Apple Watch, widgets, Siri and themes are free.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, select Stretch Break, and you can change or cancel there. A subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open the Pro screen in the app and tap “Restore purchases,” signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe.

Does the app work with Apple Watch?

Yes. The Apple Watch app lets you start and complete breaks from your wrist, and your streak stays in sync.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data stays on your device and your private iCloud — we never see it, and there are no ads or trackers. See our Privacy Policy.
